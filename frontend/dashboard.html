Analysis Services

  • Population Density

    • Calculates population per unit area to assess concentration.

  • Public Transport Station Coverage

    • Evaluates how well public transport stations cover & serve the selected area.

  • Facility Accessibility Index

    • Determines accessibility to key services (e.g., schools, hospitals) based on travel time or distance.

  • Heat Index

    • Analyzes temperature raster data to identify heat intensity and hotspots.

  • Vegetation Density

    • Measures the proportion of green areas relative to the total land area.

  • NDVI

    • Assesses the health and vitality of vegetation using satellite-derived indices.

  • Safety / Crime Density

    • Maps and analyzes the concentration of crime incidents across the area.

  • Traffic Analysis

    • Examines traffic patterns and congestion levels within the selected region.

  • Informal Settlement Pattern Analysis

    • Detects informal settlement patterns from satellite imagery using texture irregularity, edge density, and built-up crowding metrics.

  • Air Quality Index

    • Retrieves and evaluates air quality data for the defined area of interest.

  • Future Expansion Suitability

    • Identifies the most suitable areas for development by scoring and combining all prior analysis results.
